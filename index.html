Inbound Driver Check-In

  1. Enter the Facility:
    • Enter through the main gate and drive around to the Receiving Door.
  2. Be Prepared to Check In:
    • Bring two original signed copies of your Bill of Lading (no photocopies).
    • Ensure your seal number matches the BOL and the trailer number also matches.
    • Make sure you arrive on time for your scheduled appointment.
  3. Appointment Rules:
    • Drop loads may check in 24 hours in advance.
    • Live loads may check in 12 hours in advance.
    • These timeframes are strict — no exceptions.
  4. If You’re Not Scheduled:
    • If you’re not in our system, we will ask you to contact your dispatch.
    • You must be scheduled through Transplace or by posting a PGI (Post Goods Issue).
  5. Professional Conduct:
    • Please be respectful and patient — some days are busier than others.
    • If you are late, we will try to work you in as soon as possible, but we must honor scheduled appointments.
    • If time is an issue, contact dispatch to reschedule.

Outbound Driver Check-In

  1. Prepare to Check In:
    • Pick-up number (450, 0100, 904, or 905)
    • Carrier name
    • Trailer number
    • Phone number
    • Delivery city
    • Company you drive for
  2. Prepare Your Trailer:
    • Slide tandems to the back
    • Open trailer doors
    • Place a load strap at the rear
    • Disconnect air lines
  3. Wait for Loading Signal:
    • RED LIGHT = We are loading you
    • GREEN LIGHT = Loading is complete
  4. After Loading:
    • Drive to the white box
    • Come inside the Shipping Office
    • Bring your driver’s license
    • Sign your BOLs (Bill of Lading)
  5. Final Trailer Check:
    • We will inspect your trailer
    • We’ll place the packing slip in the back
    • We will seal your trailer
