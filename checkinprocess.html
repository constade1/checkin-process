Driver Check-In Process

Welcome to our distribution center. Please follow the steps below to complete your check-in:

  • Enter through Gate A and stop at the Security Booth.
  • Show your ID and Bill of Lading to the guard.
  • You will be assigned a dock number.
  • Proceed to the assigned dock and wait for instructions.
  • Stay in your truck unless otherwise directed.
  • Once unloading/loading is complete, return to the booth for your paperwork.

For assistance, contact the yard supervisor at extension 105.
